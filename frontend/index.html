How It Works

The recommendation engine follows a structured machine learning pipeline to deliver professional-grade suggestions.

📥

1. Data Preprocessing

The system collects raw grocery transaction logs and structures them into a usable format for the recommendation engine.

🧹

2. Data Cleaning

Duplicates are removed, product synonyms are mapped, and noise is filtered out to ensure high data quality and recommendation accuracy.

📊

3. Model Evaluation

The engine calculates confidence and lift metrics to validate patterns and deliver the most relevant product suggestions.
